The plain SMB share answers every open, seek, and scrub with a fresh round trip to the NAS, because nothing is cached: 1,390 round trips and 38 seconds of waiting over a home VPN. JuiceMount crosses the wire once per new block and answers every repeat from the local SSD cache: 20 round trips and 7.3 seconds for the same minute.
